Muʻo in San Francisco

mahalo nui loa

In ʻŌlelo Hawaiʻi, muʻo is the young leaf bud beginning to open. At Purple Maiʻa, they are the younger members of our ʻohana, the next generation of builders, designers, and overall talent.

Like all muʻo, they need the right conditions, people, spaces, and moments to really blossom.

By welcoming them into your spaces, you showed our muʻo that people who look like them, and come from where they come from, hold space in these incredible companies, ultimately showing them that there is a place for them in this industry too.

They came home full. Good food helped, but that was mostly you.

Mahalo.

With aloha, the Purple Maiʻa ʻohana
